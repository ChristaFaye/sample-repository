CHECKING OFF MY BUCKET LIST

“Adventure is worthwhile in itself.” ― Amelia Earhart

I am a Filipino who grew up in a tight knit family. Hence, my parents are really strict and don't normally allow me to go partying or some kind of. But, I do not regret that as I am enjoying now what I have now because of them. Growing up, i used to just hang out with few friends and enjoy little chats over a cup of coffee. As I started working, I saw people of the same age who experienced a lot and I can compare myself to them, seeing myself as a 'dead kid'.

I thought bucket list is only for those people who are adventurous and outgoing. Me, as an introvert never thought of creating one. Just because my only plan on holidays is to curl on my bed, read some books, browse the internet while sipping a hot coffee. But, guess what? Never too late to have one, I’d say. If you’re the same person as me before, just browsing on your phone while sipping your coffee, maybe you can join me checking off my bucket list, then. Hope this can inspire you as well to finish that cup of coffee and start exploring! Shall we?
